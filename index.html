What We Do

HTYF has worked across hundreds of communities in three (3) Nigerian states - FCT, Plateau and Enugu, enhancing the quality of the lives of to engage, involve, collaborate, and empower vulnerable groups through evidence-based, gender sensitive programs in Health, Civic Engagement, Empowerment and Orphans & Vulnerable Children (OVC). Using advocacy, awareness creation, service delivery and many best practices, we improve the health, resilience, and protection of our target beneficiaries.

Health

Promoting the survival of mothers & children and Basic Health Care Provision for every Nigeria.

Empowerment

Promoting access for vulnerable groups to culturally acceptable gender sensitive & standardized economic strengthening activities.

Civic Engagement

Promoting peace building, rights & responsibility, voter’s education, value reorientation and de-radicalization of Youths.

OVC

Improving the wellbeing of Orphans & Vulnerable Children (OVC) and their caregivers using world best practice.

About Us

Heal The Youth Foundation (HTYF) is a registered Nigerian Non-Governmental Organization (CAC/IT/NO 18565) who believe in interventions that build sustainable structures that transform the lives of Orphans and Vulnerable Children (OVC), Women and Youths. Our work is guided by a five (5) year Strategic Plan that outlines our vision, core values, goals and objectives for 2019-2023. The Plan provides a detailed blueprint for action, tracking progress and clarifying roles and responsibilities.

Children thrive better within their communities and your donations equip them with the adequate skills, knowledge and capacity to cater to their health, nutritional and socio-economic needs. You make all the difference through your continued dedication and support.
