Empowering Lives Positively.

What We do

In about two decades of existence, HTYF through four main focal themes – Health, Civic Engagement, Empowerment and Orphans & Vulnerable Children – has evolved and grown to manage multiple local and international donor funding. This has given us the experience to implement National, and sub-national health, social and economic programs for the most vulnerable groups in Nigeria.

Support-A-Child Initiative

Since 2010 HTYF has been able to provide educational, psychosocial, water/sanitation and hygiene, food & nutritional, shelter Health and economic strengthening support through the Support-A-Child-Initiative (SACI) program.

Youth Safe Space

Young people share of the African population makes them integral to the continents socio-economic and political development and Youth Safe Space is designed to address the “sexual reproductive and mental health” and “the personal development” needs of young people and empower them with knowledge and skills to actualize their life’s potentials, visions and goals.

Dream Alive Program

The Dream Alive Program aims to facilitate access for Entrepreneurs to affordable and equitable financial services while building their entrepreneurial competence, strengthening their economic activities, and reducing locally specific barriers that young women entrepreneurs face.
